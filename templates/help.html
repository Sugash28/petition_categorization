Help & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a grievance?

To submit a grievance, click on the "Submit Grievance" button on the homepage and fill out the required details in the form.

How can I track the status of my grievance?

You can track the status of your grievance by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Grievances" section.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
